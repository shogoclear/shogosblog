About me

I was born and raised in Japan.
Interested in travelling, looking for hotels with a good atmosphere, studying English and café hopping.
Graduated from university in 2021 and then went to Toronto to study abroad, where I entered Seneca college four months later.
My major at university was Sustainability studies and my post-graduate degree at college was International Business Management.
From there, I got into programming and studied to create a blog. And now I have created a blog and tried and tested to provide useful information on living abroad.

This website intends to post diaries and blogs. For the diary, I intend to write about interesting things from my casual life. For the blog, I would like to post information about life in Toronto, nice cafes or restaurants, and also introduce Japan to some international viewers. As this site is part of my hobby, the information may contain some of my biases.
